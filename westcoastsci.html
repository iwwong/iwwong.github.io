NOTE

Completed as the Marketing and Social Media Assistant at Westcoast SCI

Westcoast SCI is an active-based physiotherapy clinic that personalizes their care, one-on-one, based on the patient's needs. I was tasked with the responsibility of creating posters that resonate with one of their target audiences, nurses. This poster has been displayed in nurse's staff rooms at local hospitals and practices.

IDEATION

As a way to advertise Westcoast SCI's services to a targeted population, they wanted to highlight methods of physiotherapy that can help with certain pains and aches that a nurse may have from their jobs. Rather than simply listing each of the methods, I wanted to create some eye-catching visuals that would hopefully grab the attention of a nurse passing by the room during their break.

I drew each of the images in Illustrator while referencing actual photographs.

REFLECTION

I chose to create the illustrations in a more detailed style for this poster so I really enjoyed this process because it allowed me to spend more time practicing my illustration skills in Illustrator. Since I am not knowledgeable in physiotherapy, the physiotherapists at Westcoast SCI were able to assist me in making sure my information was correct and the images I was presenting were accurate.
